THE AD HOC WINERY

The Cellar is one of our vaunts

Our winelist exhibits around a 1000 wine labels, most of are available in various vintages, magnum formats, and around 30 wines are available by the glass ( of which red full bodied wine bottles are preserved and poured from an enomatic machine).

We carry Italian distinguished and renowned wines that are awarded on the national and international scene: Gaja, Quintarelli, Dal forno, Valentini, Franz Hass, Biondisanti, Sassicaia, Camarcanda; La Spinetta, Lupicaia, Massetto, Solaia, e Salvioni, among others.
Our 2 in-house sommeliers, passionate wine lovers, are happy to advise and guide you to an Adhoc choice...
