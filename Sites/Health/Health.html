HEALTH

Our raw materials, of undisputed quality

In the preparation of our dishes we pay particular attention to health by going through:
- the enhancement of the territory

- the quality and freshness of the raw materials

- the small and large needs of our customers (intolerant, allergic, celiac)

- daily homemade baking

- the daily preparation of fresh homemade pasta and pulled by hand

- the seasoning of the pillows and hams in our tuff cellars

- certification of the quality and origin of meat and fish

- the freshness and authenticity of dairy products

- the use of only extra virgin olive oil

and much more in order to guarantee the achievement of our objectives of quality, territoriality, short supply chain.
